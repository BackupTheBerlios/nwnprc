Now you have a copy of the PRC downloaded to your desktop.

The next step is to extract it from the package.

 To do so simply double click on it. You may see a warning message like this:



Assuming you don't think there are any viruses in it (there shouldn't be), click the "Run" button to allow it to proceed.

Next, the installer window should appear:



If it has the correct NWN directory, press "Install".

If it cannot find your NWN directory (may have an error message about the registry), try uninstalling and re-install in NWN and all of its expansions.

After you press the "Install" button, it will extract the files and you will eventually see a screen like this:



Make sure that checkbox is ticked and the click "Finish".

The next step is associating the PRC with your modules.
