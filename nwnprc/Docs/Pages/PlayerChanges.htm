Changes to existing content

back to Players.

In order to maintain maximum compatibility and ease of use, where possible content has been added, rather than changed. The PnP Shifter class is an example of this; rather than overwriting Bioware's version, an alternative was created. However, at some points it makes sense to replace or alter existing content. 

Assassin

In addition to their normal abilities, assassins now receive an extra feat at level 1 designed to more closely follow the Pen 'n' Paper rules for Death Attack.

True Death Attack
Type of feat: Class
Prerequisite: Level 1 Assassin
Specifics: After studying your target for 3 rounds, you have 3 rounds to slay them vs DC 10 + Assassin level + Int Mod.
*Note - using this feat will remove your stealth, you have 6 seconds to become stealthy again in order to continue studying the target. If anybody knows a way around this give me a shout. As per PnP the weapon can only be melee, so even though it is possible in code for ranged weapons it is not included..

How it works: Target (longest range in the game) what you want to kill, go invisible or stealth, move closer into melee range of the target. You have to study the target for 3 rounds(18 seconds), and while studying you cant go out of stealth, or attack. Once the 3 rounds are up your primary weapon or gloves if unarmed are given a temporary Slay Race (race of the target) for 3 rounds at the above DC.

Arcane Archer

This class has had its prerequisites modified to make it more widely avaliable. Now, instead of "Arcane Spellcasting Level 1", you need to have at least 1 level in one of the following classes: Sorcerer, Bard, Wizard, Assasin, and Harper Scout.

Turn Undead

Bioware's Turn Undead script has been replaced with Wes Baran's improved Turn Undead script. It is available on the vault Here. It follows the same rules as the bioware script with some critical differences. Evil characters that turn undead do not turn or destroy undead, they will stun or dominate them. A character can only dominate a number of hit dice worth of undead equal to their turning level, any above that will be stunned instead. Dominated undead will be freed when their master rests.

 

Power Attack / Improved Power Attack

These have been separated into sub-radials so you can select exactly which degree of accuracy/power trade-off you wish.

Spell Changes

Bigby's Crushing Hand

Changed to correctly calculate grapple check for the target as d20 + BAB + strength modifier + size modifier.


Bigby's Grasping Hand

Changed to correctly calculate grapple check for the target as d20 + BAB + strength modifier + size modifier.


Firebrand

Changed to allow reflex save for half damage as per PnP rules.


Mage Armor

Changed to follow PnP rules of being a +4 armor bonus.


Summon Creature VII - IX

Changed to radial menus to choose the creature being summoned and added earth elementals, so all 4 elemental types can be summoned. Note: This change may affect the AI of NPC casters, generally they cant cope with radial spells.


Harm

Does 10HP/level. Capped at 150 HP.


Heal

Does 10HP/level. Capped at 150 HP.


Bombardment

Raised cap to 20d8.


Earthquake

25% chance to be forced to make a reflex save (DC20) against death. All must make reflex save (DC15) or be knocked down for 3 rounds.


Greater Dispel

Raised cap to 20.


Mass Heal

Does 10HP/level. Capped at 250 HP.


Greater Restore

Does 10HP/level. Capped at 250 HP.


Timestop

Duration is 1d4+1 rounds


Epic Spells

The PRC now contains Boneshank's Epic Spellcasting System. As part of this all of the Bioware Epic Spells have been re-written and added to, though the Bioware spells have the same name and effects.
