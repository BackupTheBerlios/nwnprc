RAL - Random Area Linker

The aim of this is to use doorways and other objects to connect areas of a similar theme into a random collection of areas, and to maintain those links untill the areas are reset. It has a few other checks too, to make sure an area doesnt link to itself for example, but otherwise it is pretty straightforward (compared to some of the other systems anyway).
