NWAngband - Features

At its core, NWAngband is composed of several interlinked systems which feed content to and from each other. These are known as:

  • RIG - Random Item Generator
  • REG - Random Encounter Generator
  • RAD - Random Area Decorator
  • RAL - Random Area Linker
  • RQG - Random Quest Generator
In addition, there are a number of other smaller systems dealing with other aspects. Some of these are:
  • DRDT - Directed Randomised Data Tables
  • PRS - Perception Reputation System
  • REF - Random Equipment Functions
  • RNG - Random Name Generator
  • Ambience Simulation
  • Swarm Management
  • Streamlined Town Interface
As well as all these custom made systems, NWAngband uses some of the best systems that the NWN community has made:
  • PRC - Player Resource Consortium
  • Sunjammers Tilemagic
Plus as well as all the gameplay elements listed above, NWAngband makes use of custom content from various community creators; including, but not limited to:
  • Tilesets and doors from CTP - Community Tileset Pack
  • Various creature and other models from the NWN community collected through the PRC Companion
  • The Tir Na N'og (TNO) tilesets from DLA
